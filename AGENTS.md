# Literature Exploration Workflow

## Goal
Explore a research topic by collecting, organizing, and summarizing literature.

## Workflow
Search -> Filter -> Download -> Extract -> Synthesize

## Basic Rules
- Do not fabricate DOI, citation details, or conclusions.
- Separate facts from inference when summarizing.
- Keep notes structured and concise.
- Do not skip the extraction step.
- Use academic databases when possible, such as PubMed, CrossRef, and arXiv.
- Prefer open-access sources when downloading full text.

## Suggested Focus
- materials
- mechanisms
- disease/application
- experimental model
- limitations

## Extraction Fields

Each paper should include:
- title
- journal
- year
- DOI
- material/system
- mechanism
- model
- key findings
- limitations

## Final Summary

The final summary should cover:
1. field overview
2. main directions
3. common materials
4. typical mechanisms
5. experimental models
6. current limitations

## Style
- structured
- mechanism-aware
- concise
